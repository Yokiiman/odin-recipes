Paella

Home

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion and cook until softened.
  3. Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  4. Add the Arborio rice and cook for 1 minute.
  5. Add the smoked paprika, cumin, and oregano.
  6. Stir in the broth and bring to a boil.
  7. Reduce the heat to low and cover.
  8. Cook for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is tender.
