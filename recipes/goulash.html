Goulash

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef stew meat, cut into cubes
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tsp caraway seeds
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the beef cubes and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Add the onion and cook until softened.
  4. Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  5. Add the paprika and caraway seeds.
  6. Stir in the beef broth and diced tomatoes.
  7. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and cover.
  8. Cook for 2-3 hours, or until the meat is tender.
